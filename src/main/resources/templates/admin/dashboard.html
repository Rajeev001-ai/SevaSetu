Platform Overview

Welcome to the Admin Control Panel. This dashboard gives you complete control over the Local Service platform. From here, you can monitor users, helpers, service bookings, and overall system performance in real-time.

As an administrator, your responsibility is to ensure smooth operations between customers and service providers. You can verify helpers, manage active services, track booking statuses, and monitor revenue generated across the platform.

The statistics above provide a quick snapshot of current activity. You can dive deeper into each module using the navigation menu on the left. Each section is designed to give you detailed insights and full administrative control.

Admin Responsibilities
  • Approve or reject helper registrations
  • Monitor and manage user activity
  • Track booking lifecycle from request to completion
  • Resolve disputes and handle escalations
  • Ensure data accuracy and system security
System Management
  • Manage available service categories
  • Enable or disable helpers when required
  • Monitor platform earnings and commissions
  • Analyze performance trends
  • Maintain overall service quality
Important Note

This admin panel is designed to scale as the platform grows. As new services, helpers, and users are added, the dashboard will automatically reflect updated data. Always ensure helpers are verified before allowing them to accept bookings to maintain trust and service quality.

Regularly review booking statuses and earnings reports to ensure transparency and smooth business operations.
